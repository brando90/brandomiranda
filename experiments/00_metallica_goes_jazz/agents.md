## Quick Tips for Generation

Generate **2-3 takes per prompt** — Suno is inconsistent and the second or third often nails the vibe better than the first. If a take comes out sounding too modern smooth-jazz (a common Suno failure mode for jazz), regenerate with **"acoustic instruments only, no synthesizers, vintage analog recording"** appended.

For the Parker bebop prompt specifically, if 200 BPM ends up too chaotic, drop to 160 BPM — Suno sometimes loses coherence at extreme tempos.
