## Selection criteria to target

The public program overview says eligible candidates are students currently pursuing a bachelor's, master's, or Ph.D. in quantitative fields, with particular interest in mathematics, computer science, physics, statistics, or related areas. For paper-publishing research conferences such as ICML or NeurIPS, preference is given to applicants who have submitted at least one paper to that conference. The overview also says the panel considers academic performance, publication record, previous awards, presentation/involvement, potential conference impact, and relevance to Citadel's work.

The terms also say the application can request personal information, resume, representative work experience, relevant publications/conference submissions, and other applicant content.
